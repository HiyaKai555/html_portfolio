About Me

Hi, I’m Terri—just a slightly chaotic human navigating life with a keyboard in one hand and a curiosity-driven heart in the other.

I’m a Cognitive Science enthusiast with one eye on psychology and the other on artificial intelligence. I spend my days trying to understand what makes humans tick—and my nights questioning whether machines might someday do the same. (Yes, I’ve definitely cried over fictional robots. More than once.)

I’m currently juggling coding projects, Japanese language study (JLPT N2, here I come!), and a love for storytelling—whether it’s through writing, design, or just emotionally processing movies way too deeply at 2 a.m.

Movies like Warrior and Chappie made me want to fight (literally—I joined a kickboxing club as a kid) and feel (existential dread included). They pushed me to explore the blurry lines between emotion and logic, softness and strength, and what it really means to be "alive."

I believe in creating things that matter—tools, stories, apps, whatever—that help people understand themselves a little better. And maybe, if I’m lucky, I’ll build something that makes someone feel less alone.
